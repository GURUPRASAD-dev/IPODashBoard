What is IPO GMP?

IPO Grey Market Premium (GMP) indicates the unofficial market price of an IPO before its listing.

Important Points to Consider about IPO GMP:

  • GMP is not an official metric; consider other financial data as well.
  • Reflects demand and potential listing gains of an IPO.
  • Influenced by market sentiment, company fundamentals, and subscription rates.

What is Kostak Rate?

The Kostak rate is the price someone pays for an IPO application before its allotment.
